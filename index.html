Easy Balance

Have trouble remembering various USSD codes, text messages and numbers to know your balance? Leave it to us. With easy balance our aim is to provide the users a single app which can be used to check balance and mobile data. Only thing you need is a functioning finger or anything with which you can touch the phone to use the app.

Papierr

Make notes, edit them and even remove them when you want to. Papierr was designed to be a simple app to keep notes and reminders and get back to them when you need to. It is very user-friendly and considerably small in size. Papierr is constantly being improved everyday and is open source.
